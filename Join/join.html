Welcome to our coding club!

If you're passionate about technology, coding, and creating cool projects, you're in the right place.

Membership:

  • Open to all students interested in coding and technology
  • Everyone is welcome, regardless of skill/experience level
  • To join, follow the steps on the side

How to Join:

  • Join our club by Filling out the form here!
  • Contact us in the Google Classroom for any regards relating to joining the club.

Club Meeting Schedule:

We meet every Monday after school. Sometimes, we have sessions during flex block. Stay updated on our Google Classroom and announcements for any changes or special events

What to Expect:

  • Engaging coding projects and challenges.
  • Hackathon trips and school-hosted events!!
  • A collaborative learning environment.

  • A fun and supportive community of fellow coders.

Benefits of Joining:

  • Enhance your coding skills.
  • Explore new technologies and programming languages.
  • Build a portfolio of projects.
  • Connect with like-minded peers and mentors.
